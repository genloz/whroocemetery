WELCOME TO WHROO CEMETERY

This is the official website of the Whroo Cemetery Trust. This cemetery was officially created in 1860, following a number of earlier burials between 1853 and 1860. The last burial was in 1971, with official closure not announced by the Victorian Government until 2022.

The Whroo Cemetery Trust warmly acknowledges the many research efforts made to supply information, arising from the incomplete state of official cemetery records. The Genealogical Society of Victoria, Doris King, Nicola Thomson, Marion Riley, the Shire of Campaspe, James Thomson, Anita Bartlett, Christine Norris and Alan McLean are known to have applied hundreds of hours to the objective of creating the most complete and accurate listing of burials at Whroo.

LOCATION OF GRAVES

With records incomplete, numbered grave markers lost or removed over time, and some graves never identified by any form of marking beyond a mound of earth, it is only possible to display a plan which is reasonably accurate according to at least two indicators. The reverse side of the information board standing at the cemetery provides that display.
